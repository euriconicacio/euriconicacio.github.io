Alternative Approaches for Modeling Seasonal Signals from Amazon Basin GPS Time Series open source

The present research deals with different techniques of decomposition and identification of seasonal signals present in the GPS time series. These are a classical approach through Singular Spectral Analysis (SSA) implementation; and a modern approach through the Empirical Mode Decomposition (EMD) algorithm and its variations.
